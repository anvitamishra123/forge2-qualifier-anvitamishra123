name: kanban-sprint

description: Plan, build, test and report progress for the Forge 2 Kanban app.

\---



When working on the Forge 2 Kanban app:



1\. Understand the current task.

2\. Break the work into small steps.

3\. Assign implementation work to OpenClaw.

4\. Test the result.

5\. Report in this format:



\*\*What I Did\*\*

\- Completed work.



\*\*What's Left\*\*

\- Remaining work.



\*\*What Needs Your Call\*\*

\- Human decision required.
